Tomato Plant Lifecycle

Seed (0-7 days)

Description: Tomato seeds are sown indoors 6-8 weeks before the last frost date.

Watering: Keep soil consistently moist but not waterlogged.

Fertilizing: No fertilizing needed during this stage.

Germination (5-10 days)

Description: Seeds germinate at 20-25Â°C. Monitor soil moisture carefully.

Watering: Lightly water daily to keep the topsoil moist.

Fertilizing: No fertilizing yet.

Seedling Growth (10-20 days)

Description: Seedlings develop their first true leaves. Transplant to larger pots if needed.

Watering: Water every 2-3 days, ensuring the soil dries slightly between watering.

Fertilizing: Apply diluted liquid fertilizer (half strength) every 10 days.

Flowering (20-40 days)

Description: Plants develop flowers, signaling preparation for fruit production.

Watering: Water deeply 2-3 times a week, depending on soil moisture.

Fertilizing: Use a balanced fertilizer (e.g., 10-10-10) once a week.

Fruiting (40-70+ days)

Description: Fruits grow and ripen. Harvest when ripe.

Watering: Deep water weekly; avoid wetting leaves to prevent disease.

Fertilizing: Switch to high-potassium fertilizer (e.g., 5-10-10) every 2 weeks.
