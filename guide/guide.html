Tomato Care Guide

Plant Traits

Click for Details

Tomatoes are typically bushy or vining plants. They grow up to 4-6 feet tall depending on the variety. Bush tomatoes are compact, while vining types require support.

Watering Conditions

Click for Details

Tomatoes require regular watering. Aim for deep watering 1-2 times a week. Ensure soil is moist but not soggy to avoid root rot.

Soil Conditions

Click for Details

Tomatoes prefer well-drained, loamy soil. Ensure the soil is rich in organic matter for optimal growth. Sandy soil is also acceptable with added compost.
